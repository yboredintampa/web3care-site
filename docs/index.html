Privacy-first stroke-recovery companion for families and caregivers.

Inspired to help families navigating stroke recovery, Web3Care (pre-MVP) is a PWA vision for tracking vitals, meds, meals, therapy, mood, appointments, bill payment reminders, and cognitive-strengthening activities (games)—with a daily non-PHI SMS summary. SMS conveys signals only: BP status (High / Low / In-range—never numbers), bills due, upcoming appointments, and participation in cognitive games. Solana-first (~$0.91/user/year). Releases will be mirrored to Storj (optional IPFS).

Features

HIPAA-minded design (no vendor PHI, no BAAs)
We do not receive, process, or store PHI. PHI stays on user devices with client-side encryption. Because no vendors handle PHI, BAAs are not applicable.
Care tracking that sticks
BP, meds, therapy, meals, sleep, mood, notes, appointments—plus bill payment reminders and cognitive games built in.
Daily summaries (non-PHI)
SMS conveys signals only: BP status (High / Low / In-range—no numeric vitals), bills due, upcoming appointments, and participation in cognitive games. Tap to view details securely in the app.
Seedless sign-in
Email 2FA + Passkeys (Face ID/Touch ID). No seed phrases. Account abstraction for roles & session keys.
Privacy by architecture
PHI stays on user devices (client-side encrypted). On-chain: roles/receipts only—never PHI.
Verifiable releases
Frontend builds mirrored to Storj with optional IPFS, plus published SHA-256 checksums.
