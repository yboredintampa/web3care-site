Privacy-first stroke-recovery companion for families and caregivers.

Inspired by my mother’s stroke recovery. Web3Care is in development (pre-MVP)—a PWA to help families track vitals, meds, meals, therapy, mood & appointments, with a daily non-PHI SMS summary. Solana-first (~$0.91/user/year). Public releases will be mirrored to Storj (and optionally IPFS).
