Privacy

Web3Care is designed so that personal health information (PHI) stays on the user’s devices, encrypted end-to-end. We do not store PHI on centralized servers.

What we store

  • On devices: PHI is encrypted locally and used only in the app.
  • Backups you control: Optional encrypted backups to a second device, USB/NAS, or a user-owned home box.
  • Storj (public releases only): We may host frontend release artifacts on Storj for version transparency. No PHI is uploaded to Storj unless it is client-side encrypted and kept private.
  • On-chain (Solana): Roles/receipts/integrity hashes only—never PHI.

Messaging

Daily SMS/email notifications contain no PHI; they link back to the app, which decrypts locally on your device.

Analytics

Disabled by default. If enabled later, we’ll use minimal, privacy-respecting metrics and avoid collecting PHI.

Your choices

  • Export or delete your local data at any time.
  • Use your own backup destination (USB/NAS/home box).
  • Revoke devices and rotate access via the app’s security settings.
